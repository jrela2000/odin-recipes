Ingredients

  • 2 8-ounce packages cream cheese, softened
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 2 eggs, lightly beaten
  • 1/4 cup sour cream
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup graham cracker crumbs
  • 2 tablespoons butter, melted

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. In a medium bowl, mix cream cheese, sugar, eggs, sour cream and vanilla extract until smooth.
  3. In a small bowl, mix graham cracker crumbs and melted butter.
  4. Line an 8 inch springform pan with the graham cracker crust. Pour the cream cheese mixture into the pan.
  5. Bake in preheated oven for 35 to 40 minutes, or until center is almost set.
  6. Cool completely before refrigerating.
