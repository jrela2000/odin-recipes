Chicken and Sausage Gumbo

Chicken and Sausage Gumbo is a classic Creole dish that combines the flavors of the Gulf Coast with a rich roux-based broth. Here is a recipe for a classic gumbo that serves 6-8 people:

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound smoked sausage, sliced
  • 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken thighs, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 cups chopped onions
  • 1 cup chopped green bell pepper
  • 1 cup chopped celery
  • 6 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 8 cups chicken stock
  • 2 cups chopped okra
  • 3 cups cooked white rice

Directions:

  1. In a large pot or Dutch oven, heat the oil over medium-high heat.
  2. Add the flour and stir constantly, cooking for about 20-25 minutes, or until the roux turns a dark chocolate brown color.
  3. Add the onions, bell pepper, celery, and garlic to the roux and stir well. Cook for about 10 minutes, or until the vegetables are tender.
  4. Add the cayenne pepper, thyme, paprika, salt, and black pepper to the pot and stir well.
  5. Add the chicken, sausage, and chicken stock to the pot and stir well. Bring the mixture to a boil, then reduce the heat to low and simmer for about 30 minutes.
  6. Add the okra and continue to simmer for an additional 15 minutes.
  7. Serve the gumbo over cooked white rice. Enjoy!

Note: This recipe is a traditional recipe and you can adjust the spicy level and other ingredients as per your preference.
